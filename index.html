My Confession

Sweetheart Ghita,

There are nights when the world grows quiet enough for the heart to speak its truth in a single breath.

I have wandered many roads, learned many songs, but every melody I remember leads to you.
When I close my eyes I find your laughter like a warm light, and in that light, all my hours become softer.

Tonight, by the whisper of the flame, I offer you the simplest promise I can make:
to stand beside you in every small dawn and every slow dusk, to learn the map of your hands, and keep them gently.

If you will let me, darling, I will spend my days turning ordinary moments into a lifetime of tenderness.
